You Win, We Donate

Thanks to our sponsors, every 10 points you score, a $1 donation will be made to charities that combat animal cruelty, aid in the fight again climate change, and overall make the world a better place.

Charity? What about me?

We didn't forget about you. Every 50 points you score, you also earn $1.

How do I track my earnings?

Create an account, and from there you'll be able to see your total accumulated score, your contributions, and your total earnings.
